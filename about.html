About

I'm an Information Systems student continually practicing and perfecting system designs and development.

Ever since I can remember, I've always been intrigued by libraries. A happy medium where technology, people, and information are all in the same place. I felt like a kid in a candy store with limitless possibilities. Enthralled, I developed a curiosity for how people interacted with the tech world around them. This curiosity led me to study Information Systems, Informatics, and Data Science, allowing me to explore the intricacies of data, people, and ultimately Information Systems.

My background as a first-generation American has shaped my design philosophy to minimize barriers for users. This strives me to create inclusive and intuitive solutions that cater to diverse needs and perspectives
